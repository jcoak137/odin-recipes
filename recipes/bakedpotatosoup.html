Baked Potato Soup

Description

A hearty soup that will warm up any cold soul that can be its own meal or a side dish.

This baked potato soup is filling and requires minimal ingredients to make.

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bacon
  • 2/3 cup butter or margarine
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 7 cups milk
  • 4 large baked potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 1/4 cuips shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Place bacon in a large skilelt and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally until evenly browned, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Drain bacon slices on paper towels; crumble and set aside.
  3. Melt butter in a stockpot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  4. Gradually add flour, whisking until well-combined. Slowly pour in milk, whisking constantly until smooth and thickened.
  5. Add potatoes and onions. Bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Stir in cumbled bacon, Cheddar cheese, sour cream, salt to taste, and pepper. Continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  7. Take off heat and ladel into bowls to serve.
